Plan your visit

If you are joining us at Horizon Festival, don't miss out on the opportunity to indulge in the natural beauty of the Sunshine Coast.

To plan your visit go to Visit Sunshine Coast and Queensland.

Travel

Air

Fly direct to the Sunshine Coast from Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide & Auckland. For airlines, bookings and times visit the Sunshine Coast Airport.

Car, Bus, Bike or Foot

Travel by car is the easiest and most convenient means of travel throughout the Sunshine Coast.

For bus schedules, pick-up and drop-off locations, visit translink.com.au/.

Once you have made your way to the Sunshine Coast, there are endless opportunities to explore the hinterland and the coast by bicycle and foot. For insight into some of the Coast's renowned tracks and pathways visit The Sunshine Coast's Pathways, Tracks & Trails Guide. 

Accommodation

Our partners, The Grand Palais, have generously offered a 20% discount on all accommodation bookings across Horizon Festival by using promo code HORIZON2019. Valid for stays from 16th August - 8 September. 

Head to www.grandpalais.com.au to take advantage of this great offer.
