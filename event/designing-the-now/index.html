Designing the Now

Tonight's scheduled "Designing the Now" event has been postponed due to the chance of thunderstorm. Stay tuned to the The MET - Maroochydore City social channels for future dates of this 3 part series presented by SunCentral |Maroochydore - The Bright City. It's one not to be missed!

Designing the Now is the first in a three-part program presented by SunCentral – delivering the new Maroochydore City Centre.

The series examines architecture and its relationship to environment, its unique individual qualities and the people that create the places we inhabit.

The first chapter will open at Festival Central during Horizon Festival with a film screening, HOMO SAPIENS. Nominated for Best Feature Documentary, Edinburgh International Film Festival, this New York Times Critics’ Pick is a film about the finiteness and fragility of human existence, the end of the industrial age and what it means to be human.

More info

This is a free event for which you do not need a ticket. You can register your interest to receive updates about the event and important information for your attendance.

Director: Nikolaus Geyrhalter / Year: 2016 / Country: Austria / Duration: 94mins

Bar Opens: 6pm | Screening: 7pm | Industry Discussion: 8:30pm
