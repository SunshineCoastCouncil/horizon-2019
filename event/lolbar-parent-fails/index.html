LoLbar - Parent Fails

LoLbar - Parent Fails

Presented by Solbar

BACK BY POPULAR DEMAND!! (!?how many bad parents are out there?!)

Using your kid as an excuse to stay home? Find playing with your toddler a little boring? Jealous your friends ask about them, not you? Trying too hard to impress your 10 year old? We’ve been there!

These things are usually kept under wraps, you know societal expectations, or just generally being a super parent.... but it's time we unleash! All exposed, no holding back... Bloody kids!

Don’t have kids? We’ll tell you what it’s really like. 

JOIN JASMINE FAIRBAIRN, ANNA BRENNAN, CHRISTIAN LUTZ, ANDY THOMPSON & CAROLYN MANDERSLOOT FOR A NIGHT OF **HILARIOUS** PARENTAL HONESTY
