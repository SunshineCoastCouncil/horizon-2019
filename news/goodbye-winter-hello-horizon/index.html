Goodbye Winter. Hello Horizon.

Share

Craving sunny days and balmy nights? Bid the cold farewell for a trip to the Sunshine Coast for Horizon Festival. Blow off Winter, jump in the ocean and check out our amazing program of art, music, performance, film, art + tech, words + ideas and workshops.

GETTING HERE

Fly direct to the Sunshine Coast from Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide & Auckland. For airlines, bookings and times visit the Sunshine Coast Airport.

From Adelaide
Jetstar provides daily services from Adelaide, and three weekly services to Adelaide from the Sunshine Coast.
To book visit: booknow.jetstar.com

From Auckland
Air New Zealand provides seasonal direct flights between Auckland and the Sunshine Coast, with 4 services per week between 6 July – 14 October 2018
To book visit: airnewzealand.co.nz

From Melbourne
Jetstar and Virgin both offer daily direct flights to and from Melbourne, with 4 flights a day on average. To book visit: booknow.jetstar.com and virginaustralia.com.au

From Sydney
Jetstar, Virgin and Qantas all offer daily direct flights to and from Sydney, with 5 flights a day on average.
To book visit: booknow.jetstar.com // virginaustralia.com.au // qantas.com

To see the Sunshine Coast Airport’s full flight schedule, click here.

WHERE TO STAY

From beachside resorts to hinterland hideaways, there’s many options to choose from.

The Grand Palis
Our partners, The Grand Palais, have generously offered a 20% discount on all accommodation bookings across Horizon Festival. Valid for stays from 16th August - 8 September.

Head to www.grandpalais.com.au to take advantage of this great offer.

Airbnb
Looking for something a little more unique? Discover entire homes, private rooms and country retreats perfect for any trip on Airbnb.

MUST DO

While our program is packed with over 100 events and workshops, there are a few must-see events you can’t miss! Check out A Galaxy of Suns, Mountain, The Cold Record, POINT, Snake Sessions, [In]Place and Blak Social.

There’s plenty of free events on offer too, check out 10 things you can do for free during Horizon here. Our program is packed full of family-friendly activities for the perfect family day out.

If you’re looking to get creative, there are a number of great workshops you can participate in. Check out our Learn at the Gardens program, including mass life drawing and a kite-making workshop. There’s also a Fantastic Creatures workshop being held at the Caloundra Regional Gallery; Sydney-based artist Gerwyn Davies will lead you through the construction of a fantastic costumed creature before acting out your creation for your own photographic portrait. While you’re there, check out the Sunshine Coast Art Prize exhibition, one of the most dynamic and prestigious visual arts awards in Australia.

It’s no secret that the Sunshine Coast is one of the most beautiful parts of Australia. Our stunning beaches are back-dropped by rolling mountains, and every town is sprinkled with incredible cafes and bars where you can enjoy delicious local produce and live emerging talent. To help you enjoy the best of what both Horizon Festival and the Sunshine Coast has to offer, we’ve put together curated itineraries for one of our Festival hubs; Maroochydore, Nambour, Caloundra and the Maroochy Bushland Botanic Gardens. Grab your friends or family and set off for the day to explore!

Check out Visit Sunshine Coast for more.
