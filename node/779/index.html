POINT

POINT

Presented by QMusic

QMusic, Sunshine Coast Creative Alliance and Horizon Festival is pleased to announce the lineup for the first annual POINT. Taking over The Events Centre, Caloundra for the ultimate party, POINT is an immersive event featuring multi-art projections, installations and some of the country’s most exciting emerging and established music acts, especially for the Coast's young people.

This year Hilltop Hoods collaborator and inspirational Melbourne artists Adrian Eagle will headline the event ahead of Gold Coast rising star DVNAwho has clocked up more than 1 millions streams across her recent releases. Brisbane funk disco 6 piece First Beige, psychedelic jazz artists Sycco and Sunny Coasts very own JJJ Unearthed Big Pineapple opener Dorah Jacson round out the lineup for 2019.

POINT is curated by QMusic for young people as part of the 2019 Horizon Festival program. Grab your mates, make some new ones and wrap your ears around this all-star line-up.
